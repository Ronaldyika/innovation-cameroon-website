About Innovation Cameroon

Founded in 2019 by Nzitouo Gaetan and Bonam Osene, Innovation Cameroon (formerly Dopetech) envisions building future professionals who excel and contribute to community development through innovative technological solutions.

Mission

To bridge the gap between education and the professional world by offering practical experiences, mentorship, and industry-aligned projects.

Vision

A thriving ecosystem where learners transition into impactful professionals, powering innovation across Cameroon and beyond.

Values

Excellence

We pursue high standards in learning, building, and collaboration.

Innovation

We solve real community challenges with creative, technology-driven solutions.

Community

We foster an inclusive environment where everyone can grow and contribute.
